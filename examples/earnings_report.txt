InnovateCorp Q3 2025 Earnings Report

The star of this quarter was undoubtedly the 'QuantumLeap' AI division, which saw its revenue triple, driving the majority of the company's growth. Overall revenue hit $500 million.

Looking ahead, the company announced a major strategic push into the European market, slated for Q1 2026. A new AGI safety research lab is also a top priority.
